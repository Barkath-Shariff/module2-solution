Tom Cruise

his article is about the American actor. For other people named Tom Cruise, see Tom Cruise (disambiguation)

Thomas Cruise Mapother IV (born July 3, 1962), better known by the stage name Tom Cruise, is an American actor and producer. He has received various accolades for his work, including three Golden Globe Awards and three nominations for Academy Awards. With a net worth of $570 million as of 2020,[1] he is one of the highest-paid actors in the world.[2] His films have grossed over $4 billion in North America and over $10.1 billion worldwide,[3] making him one of the highest-grossing box office stars of all time

Early life

Cruise was born Thomas Cruise Mapother IV in Syracuse, New York, on July 3, 1962,[5] the son of special education teacher Mary Lee (née Pfeiffer; 1936–2017) and electrical engineer Thomas Cruise Mapother III (1934–1984).
